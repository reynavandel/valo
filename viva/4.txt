1. What is the aim of Experiment 4?

Answer:
To improve a Generative AI prompt using word embeddings and compare the responses generated for the original and enriched prompts.

2. What are word embeddings?

Answer:
Word embeddings are numerical vector representations of words that capture semantic meaning and relationships.

3. Which word embedding model is used?

Answer:
GloVe (glove-wiki-gigaword-100)

4. What is GPT-2?

Answer:
GPT-2 is a Transformer-based Generative AI model used for text generation.

5. Which library is used to load GPT-2?

Answer:
The Transformers library by Hugging Face.

6. What is the purpose of pipeline()?

Answer:
It loads a pre-trained NLP model for a specific task such as text generation or sentiment analysis.

7. What does pipeline("text-generation") do?

Answer:
It loads the GPT-2 model for generating text from a given prompt.

8. Why is GloVe used in this experiment?

Answer:
GloVe is used to find semantically similar words that enrich the original prompt.

9. What is prompt enrichment?

Answer:
Prompt enrichment is the process of improving a prompt by replacing important words with semantically similar words.

10. Why do we replace the keyword?

Answer:
To provide a richer context so that the AI model generates more meaningful and detailed responses.

11. What does most_similar() do?

Answer:
It retrieves the top similar words to the given keyword using word embeddings.

12. What does word_tokenize() do?

Answer:
It splits the input sentence into individual words (tokens).

13. Why is string.punctuation used?

Answer:
To remove punctuation marks before comparing words.

14. What is the purpose of replace_keyword_in_prompt()?

Answer:
It replaces the specified keyword in the prompt with its most similar word from GloVe.

15. What is the purpose of generate_response()?

Answer:
It sends the prompt to GPT-2 and returns the generated text.

16. Why are the original and enriched prompts compared?

Answer:
To observe whether prompt enrichment improves the quality and detail of the generated response.

17. How is the comparison done?

Answer:
By comparing:

Response length
Number of sentences/details
Overall relevance
18. What are the applications of prompt engineering?

Answer:

Chatbots
AI assistants
Content generation
Question answering
Text summarization
19. Explain the flow of Experiment 4.

Answer:

Import required libraries.
Load GloVe word embeddings.
Tokenize the prompt.
Replace the keyword with a similar word.
Load the GPT-2 model.
Generate responses for both prompts.
Compare the outputs.
20. What is the main advantage of prompt enrichment?

Answer:
Prompt enrichment provides better context, resulting in more relevant, meaningful, and detailed AI-generated responses.
